Privacy Policy for VrGallery

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VrGallery ("we", "our", or "the App") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe VR experience.

1. Data Collection (VRC.Privacy.1)

VrGallery only accesses the minimum data required to provide its core functionality. We may access:

  • User ID and Profile Information: To identify you within the VR environment.
  • Avatars: To represent your visual identity in the gallery.
  • Age Group: To ensure compliance with age-appropriate content guidelines.

2. Use of Data (VRC.Privacy.2)

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your username and profile picture within the App's user interface.
  • To customize the VR gallery experience based on your profile.
  • We do not use this data for advertising or any other secondary purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure (VRC.Privacy.3)

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties.
  • Local Processing: Most data is processed locally on your Meta Horizon device.

4. Data Retention and Deletion (VRC.Privacy.4)

Users have the right to control their data:

  • Data Deletion: Since we do not store your personal data on external servers, deleting the App from your device will remove any locally stored preferences.
  • Request Deletion: If you wish to request the deletion of any data associated with your account, please contact us at: wupolung@teamail.ltu.edu.tw.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:
